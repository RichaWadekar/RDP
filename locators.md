# RainyDay Parents Login & OTP Locators

- **Continue Button:** `button:has-text("Continue")`
- **Email Input:** `input[type="email"]`
- **OTP Input:** `input[type="tel"]`
- **Yopmail Login Input:** `#login`
- **Yopmail Inbox Iframe:** `#ifinbox`
- **Yopmail Mail Row:** `div.m` (inside inbox iframe)
- **Yopmail Mail Body Iframe:** `#ifmail`
- **Yopmail Mail Body:** `body` (inside mail iframe)
